Privacy Policy for AR Draw FREE

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

AR Draw FREE ("the App") is developed and published by Erdem Ozdemir.

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

Information We Collect

AR Draw FREE does not require user registration and does not create user accounts.

The App does not directly collect, store, or process personal information such as:

Name
Email address
Phone number
Postal address
Photos
User-generated content
Advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect and process certain information automatically, including:

Advertising Identifier (IDFA on iOS or Advertising ID on Android)
Device information
IP address
Approximate location derived from IP address
Ad interaction data
Diagnostic and performance information

When required by applicable law, the App requests user consent before personalized advertising is shown. If permission is granted through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, personalized advertisements may be displayed. If permission is denied, non-personalized advertisements may be shown instead.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:

Google Privacy Policy

and

Google Advertising Technologies

Data Storage

AR Draw FREE does not maintain user accounts and does not store personal user data on its own servers.

Third-Party Services

Children's Privacy

AR Draw FREE is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and appropriate action will be taken.

Your Privacy Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding the processing of your personal information.

You may:

Reset or limit your advertising identifier through your device settings.
Opt out of personalized advertising through available platform settings.
